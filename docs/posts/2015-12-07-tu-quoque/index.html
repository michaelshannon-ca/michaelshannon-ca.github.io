Fumbling with Haskell, Hakyll and Github.io

Posted on December 7, 2015 by Michael Shannon

Well, it was time to get with this millenium and contribute to the communal realm by publishing publically. I’m not ready for the quagmire that is social media ( I still see it as an excessive sacrifice of privacy that brings more harm than good ) but I wanted to proffer my own experiences to the public discourse and even this is more public than I’m completely comfortable with.

Non the less, I wasn’t ready to give my bits and bytes to other platforms and I waste too much of my life maintaning web-ish infrastructure for other projects so the free hosting available via GitHub.io was key. And, since I enjoy Haskell, Hakyll was my chosen alternative to the dominate Jekyll.
